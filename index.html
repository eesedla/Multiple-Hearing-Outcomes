Do outcomes escalate for repeat offenders?

146 officers faced 3 or more disciplinary hearings between 2017 and early 2026. Suspension remains the most common result no matter how many times an officer has been charged.

Source: Signal Cleveland analysis of 1,663 Cleveland Division of Police disciplinary hearing records, 2017 to early 2026. A single hearing can produce multiple outcomes across different charges. Percentages reflect share of total outcomes at each hearing.
